About our Little India

Our story

Little India, in Calabassas is a Middle Eastern restaurant from Chefs Ori Menashe and Parvaneh Gergis. With family roots in India Ori and Parvaneh have always wanted to open a restaurant that showcases the cuisines of their family lineages, bringing together the flavors and dishes they grew up with.

The family

Ori and Parvaneh were both born in the Los Angeles area – Parvaheh was raised in Southern California, while Ori and his family moved to India where he spent his formative years.

In 2001, Ori returned to Los Angeles. He landed his first kitchen job at an Indian cafe, before working at Lal Mirch — the restaurant where Ori and Parvaneh, who was a hostess, met for the first time.

In 2012, Ori and Parvaneh pursued their dream of opening their first restaurant, Little India. With a background in the field of interior design, Parvaneh played a key role in the design of the restaurant. When not in the kitchen, Ori and Parvaneh can be found spending time with their daughter, Saffron.

The Restaurant

We have almost 20 years of experience in making delicious traditional dishes, with recipes from the North and the South of India. Our hard work has paid off by winning the recognition of LA Times & Daily News as the best Indian restaurant in Southern California, and being critically acclaimed by AAA Magazine, LA Times and Daily News.

Once you enter our doors, you can feel the cozy and warm environment that only a family owned restaurant can offer. In Anarbargh, each member is committed to put hard work and dedication in serving our customers in the best way.
